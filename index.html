My language, my heritage, my dignity!

The African Languages Week is a perpetual event, established by the African Union, to commemorate the relevance of African languages and cultures as a factor for peace, prosperity, and integration across the continent and the diaspora.

Goal: Empowering African languages and promoting their use in all domains of society, from education to digital platforms.

Impact: Unlocking economic opportunities, advancing educational equity, and fostering social transformation within communities.
